Refections on Shareef's talk

So here's what I thought...

- What's your take on the DBC / EDA experience?

It seems like it fills a valuable niche, and enables a wide variety of people to bring their other (i.e. non-tech)skills to the tech industry.

- What are your impressions?

Relief basically. This is pretty much the way I like to work. If we see something broken, then let's fix it! I also think that my best work has often been done in a group situation. (BTW, I like kitchens!)

- How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

Easily. It's the kind that's most natural for me. I've spent a lot of time in places with the opposite style and found that it is not a way I am suited to.

- Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

No. I already did a lot of research about DBC/EDA and it just further confirms a lot of what I already thought.

- Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

Right now I'm both excited and nervous! I can't wait to develop new tech skills, relationships, social skills, etc. But can I do it? (OMG..panic..panic...!)
