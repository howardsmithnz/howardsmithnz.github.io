Cultural Blog - Triggers

How did the trigger training exercises feel?

As this stuff is quite new to me, I won't pretend that I fully understand the details of the process. I generally find it quite hard to try to relax and focus, while at the same time having to read instructions for what should happen next. I am sure that many people who are new to this find the same thing. With time and practice this is likely to become easier and more effective. That said, it was interesting to experience a little of the emotions relating to a previous event.

Did you notice any responses in yourself?

I sensed a mild change in breathing - breaths became shorter and shallower - a tensing of the stomach, and an increase in blood pressure around my head and neck.

What emotion do you want to befriend?

It would be nice if I could befriend "Irritation"! As I am frequently interrupted when doing a task, it would be very useful to be able to ignore interruptions without being agitated by "Irritation". I hope I could then quickly regain my train of thought and resume work!
