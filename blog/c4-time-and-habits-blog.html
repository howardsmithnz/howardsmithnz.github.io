Time and Habits

Reflections

  • Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

    • I scanned all the techniques and I studied more deeply those that interested me.

  • What did you find interesting?

    • The Power of Meditation - this is something I have heard a little of before, but it was nice to read a fuller explanation especially the scientific explanation of what is happening.
    • The Pomodoro Technique - I had also dabbled with doing something like this, but once again was good to see it given a bit more structure.
    • Make It Big By Starting Small - I've often found that starting a new project or task can be intimidating if one thinks that it has to be big from the start. This often leads to paralysis and inaction.
    • Create a Habit; Tiny Habits; Fogg Method - these three all share the central idea that "Action leads belief". I found it very interesting that this is what the scientific evidence shows.
    • Counter-intuitive Productivity - this was an interesting read but I'm not yet sure which of these I'll pursue.
    • Tricks For Getting Into The Zone - most of this is new to me, so I'm still evaluating this.
    • Timebox - new jargon for somthing that I already had experienced.

  • What worked for you?

    • Most of the above seem to have some merit.

  • Which will you experiment further with?

    • As above! But especially the Pomodoro Method.
